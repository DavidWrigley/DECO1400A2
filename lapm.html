Lake & Pond Management

WET has the experience in managing water bodies, regardless of the size or climatic conditions. Golf course water bodies are extremely difficult to manage and to maintain a balanced eco-system due to the high levels of nutrients that wash into the lakes from surrounding fairways & greens. WET maintain such water bodies in pristine golf courses thoughout the tropics of Queensland.

Keeping nutrients at a minimal level is a prime requirement in an eco-balanced water body. Aerobic digestion is a key element in the reduction of nutrients and keeping the water clean and healthy
