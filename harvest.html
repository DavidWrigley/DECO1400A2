Harvesting & Storage

An integral part of a sustainable irrigation system is the harvesting and storage of its water supply. Water Equipment Technology is an international designing and consultancy company that specialises in designing harvesting and storage systems of irrigation water.

Sizing storage based on harvesting opportunities and irrigation services:

• Surveys and Location Selection
• Soil Testing and Soil Treatment
• Dam Liners
• Engineered Referable Dam Wall Designs & Specification
• Pumping and Filtration Systems
• Evaporation & Seepage Measuring & Monitoring
• Anti-evaporation Techniques & Products
• Below & Above Ground Tank Selection & Specification
• Engineered designs & specification
• Gross Pollutant Trap design & specification
• Pumping and Filtration Design & Specifications
• Automatic Monitoring of Tank Water Levels
• Automatic Transfer of Water between Tanks
